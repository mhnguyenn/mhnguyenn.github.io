I’m a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems, as part of the ELLIS PhD program (50 students were selected out of 1700 candidates in 2022). My work focuses on Reinforcement Learning from both theorectical and applied perspectives, under the supervision of Adish Singla. I was a Research Intern at INRIA under the supervisions of Isabelle Guyon and Lisheng Sun-Hosoya. I obtained my Master’s degree in Big Data Management and Analytics (BDMA) joinlty from Université Paris-Saclay (France), Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (Spain), and Université Libre de Bruxelles (Belgium). I was an EMJM full-ride scholarship holder (25 students received out of 700 applications).
